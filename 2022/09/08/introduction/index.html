About me

Bonjour! I’m Xiayin Lou, a master student in Cartography and Geographic Information System in the School of Earth and Space Sciences at Peking University. My research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning, GIScience and Visualization. The core question for me is “how can geographic information benefit our society and improve our living quality?“. I want to exploit the full potential of geospatial data as well as spatially explicit models to explore the human-centered topics in the urban areas, such as human mobility, barrier-free accessibility, transportation flow, etc. Inspired by recent advance in AGI (Artificial General Intelligence), I also aim to build a unified model for the field of GIScience, which is called GeoAGI. If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to contact me!

During my leisure time, I usually enjoy trail running, marathon, etc.
